Data Collection

WordAIly uses Apple Game Center to provide features such as leaderboards and achievements. Game Center is managed by Apple and operates under Apple’s own privacy policies.

When a user signs in to Game Center, Apple may collect and store personal data such as their nickname, avatar, and gameplay activity. This data is not collected or stored by WordAIly, and is managed entirely by Apple.

For more information, please see Apple’s privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for general audiences, including children. No personal data is collected from users of any age.
